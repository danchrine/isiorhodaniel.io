1. Torilo Academy Penetration Testing

Scope

Tested toriloacademy.com including subdomains, web servers, and associated services. Testing phases included Information Gathering, Scanning, Vulnerability Assessment, Exploitation, and Post-Exploitation.

Methodology

  • WHOIS Lookup
  • DNS & Subdomain Enumeration
  • Port Scanning (Nmap, Masscan)
  • Web Vulnerability Scanning (Nikto, WPScan, Dirb)
  • WAF Detection (WAFW00F)
  • OSINT Information Gathering (Spiderfoot)
  • Packet Capture & Analysis (Wireshark)
