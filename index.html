1. Torilo Academy Penetration Testing

Scope

Tested toriloacademy.com including subdomains, web servers, and services. Phases: Information Gathering, Scanning, Vulnerability Assessment, Exploitation, Post-Exploitation.

Methodology

  • WHOIS Lookup
  • DNS & Subdomain Enumeration

  • Web Vulnerability Scanning (Nikto, WPScan, Dirb)

  • OSINT Gathering (Spiderfoot)

Findings

Low Risk Conclusion: Minimal vulnerabilities found. Recommended: regular scanning and patching.
